About DEFOQA

Our Story

DEFOQA was born from a shared passion for soccer and a commitment to quality. Our name comes from the Portuguese phrase "Defenda o que acredita" which means "Take a stand for what you believe in."

Founded by two friends who met in Australia, we bring together our love for the beautiful game and our belief in the importance of quality equipment for players of all levels. We're dedicated to delivering superior soccer balls to Australia and beyond.

Our journey is about more than just selling soccer balls – it's about inspiring courage, strength, responsibility, and the spirit of continuous challenge.

Our Mission

"DEFOQA connects, supports, and grows with people who act with conviction through soccer."

Through our products and services, we aim to convey the importance of taking action with conviction to players, children, and their families. We support everyone who has the courage to stand up for what they believe in, and we continue to challenge alongside them.
